Sele Raya Belida

Proven reserves. Seamless production.

From first gas-in at the Cantik Field to consistent oil delivery at Sungai Anggur, we deliver high-value energy through reliable infrastructure and local expertise.

Learn More

Operational Profile & Ownership

Operator

PT Sele Raya Belida 95% Participating Interest

Partner

CIEC 5% Participating Interest

Contract Term

12 December 2004 – 11 December 2034

Location

Muara Enim, South Sumatra, 95 km west of Palembang

Core Producing Assets

The Belida Block currently generates value through two flagship projects, both of which are fully integrated into Indonesia’s midstream infrastructure.

The Sungai Anggur Field Oil Focused

Status

Producing since August 2018.

Infrastructure

Features a dedicated Crude Oil Transfer Line and Custody Meter Facility.

Market

100% of production is delivered via a 20 inch transmission line to PT Pertamina’s Refinery Unit III in Plaju.

Growth

Recent expansions include the Sungai Anggur Selatan development (POD approved 31 October 2025).

The Cantik Field Gas Focused

Status

First gas achieved in July 2020.

Capacity

Features a dedicated 12 inch Gas Sales Line and Custody Meter Facility.

Market

Secured by an 11-year Gas Sales Agreement (GSA) with PT Pertagas Niaga.

Future Upside & Exploration Potential

Beyond current production, the Belida Block holds significant "low-risk" exploration targets that can be quickly monetized using existing facilities.

Sungai Anggur Selatan

Strategically located adjacent to proven commercial fields Tepus, Pandan, and Petanang Lembak Deep strengthens our growth portfolio.

These findings position Lembak Deep as a key contributor to future reserve expansion.

Lebong Area Baturaja Reservoir

Lebong Area presents a significant heavy oil opportunity with an estimated 500 MMBOIP (Original Oil in Place).

Initial well tests have successfully encountered pay zones up to 110 feet, confirming the reservoir’s substantial development potential.

Why Belida Block?

Proven Success

A track record of moving from discovery to "First Oil/Gas" efficiently.

Strategic Infrastructure

Direct tie-ins to Pertamina and Medco transmission lines ensure immediate cash flow for new discoveries.

Modern Data

All prospects are backed by modern seismic delineation, significantly reducing exploration risk.

Long Term Stability

With the PSC extending to 2034, there is a decade of runway for secondary recovery and deep exploration.
