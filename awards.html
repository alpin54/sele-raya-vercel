Quickest Exploration to Production Milestone (SKKMIGAS)

Quickest Exploration to Production Milestone (SKKMIGAS)

Sele Raya Belida proudly received the Quickest Exploration to Production Milestone award from SKK Migas in 2025.

The award was granted to Sele Raya Belida as the operator of the Belida Block in recognition of its exceptional achievement in accelerating the exploration phase through to first production within a remarkable timeframe.

This milestone reflects the company’s strong project execution, operational efficiency, and commitment to supporting Indonesia’s energy security through timely and responsible asset development. The achievement also demonstrates effective collaboration with stakeholders, adherence to regulatory standards, and a disciplined approach to risk management and field optimization.

Through this accomplishment, Sele Raya Belida reinforces its position as a reliable and performance-driven operator in Indonesia’s upstream oil and gas sector.
