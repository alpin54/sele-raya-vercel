Sele Ingredients

Redefining Ingredients, Perfecting Taste.

Transforming quality ingredients into sensory experiences you can see, smell, and taste.

Learn More

From Concept to Sensory Reality

Founded by Yasin Rahardja, Sele Ingredients was established with a clear mission: to provide high-quality ingredients at competitive prices for the Indonesian food and beverage industry. As a key subsidiary of the Sele Group, we are here to redefine industry expectations.

We don’t just supply raw materials; we transform ingredients into tangible prototypes that you can see, smell, and taste. Our team offers a wide range of solutions designed to solve your product development challenges, ensuring your products succeed both organoleptically (sensory appeal) and commercially (market viability).

Vision

To be the global pioneer in sustainable forestry by establishing the largest premium teak plantation in the world, set in the heart of Muna, Southeast Sulawesi, Indonesia.

Mission

To develop a world-class social forest management program that integrates local communities, restores the environment, and delivers premium timber through sustainable innovation.

Strategic Solutions Staying Ahead of Consumer Trends

We help your brand lead market trends through three specialized pillars optimizing formulation costs, ensuring product stability, and delivering health-focused innovation to drive your commercial success.

Smart Sweeteners

High-intensity, low-calorie solutions that deliver great taste while reducing both calories and formulation costs.

Sophisticated Additives

Advanced ingredients that ensure product stability and quality remain unchanged throughout the distribution chain.

Health & Wellness

A curated collection of natural ingredients designed to meet the demands of today's health-conscious consumers.
