Sustaining Nature for Future Generations

As part of our long-term commitment to sustainability, the Company continues to strengthen environmental initiatives focused on preserving nature and responsibly managing natural resources. These programs are designed to create meaningful impact—not only for today’s environment, but also for future generations.

Reforestation and Tree Planting Initiatives

Throughout the year, the Company carried out a large-scale tree planting program, planting more than 5,000 trees in operational areas and environmentally impacted regions. This initiative involved employees, local communities, and environmental groups. The trees were planted in critical land areas and riverbanks to help prevent soil erosion, improve air quality, and maintain ecological balance.

Sustainable Waste Management

We have also implemented an integrated waste management system based on the 3R principles—reduce, reuse, and recycle. By optimizing waste segregation across operational sites and collaborating with recycling partners, we successfully reduced landfill waste by 30% compared to the previous year.

Energy Efficiency and Emission Reduction

In support of our emission reduction targets, the Company conducts regular energy audits and has transitioned most lighting systems to energy-efficient technologies. Additionally, the use of more efficient operational equipment has significantly reduced overall energy consumption.

Environmental Education and Community Engagement

Environmental awareness plays a vital role in achieving long-term sustainability. Therefore, the Company organizes environmental education programs for students and surrounding communities, including household waste management workshops and energy-saving campaigns.

Through these initiatives, we believe sustainability is not only a responsibility but also an investment in the future. By fostering continuous collaboration between the Company, employees, and communities, we remain committed to protecting the planet and creating a greener, healthier environment for generations to come.
