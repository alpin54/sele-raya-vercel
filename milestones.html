MILESTONES

Over 50 Years of Growth, Innovation, and Impact.

From oilfield services in 1974 to a multi-sector leader today. For over 50 years, we have evolved to drive Indonesia’s growth across energy, mining, and technology.

1972

Company Establishment

We began as an oilfield services company, supporting the oil and gas industry through a range of products and services.

1984

First Field Operation

We successfully operated the Sembakung Field, North Kalimantan, onshore and produced 3,500 barrels of crude oil per day.

1994

Meruap Block Operation

We successfully operated the Meruap Block, South Sumatra, onshore and produced 3,800 barrels of crude oil per day.

The operation was conducted under a Technical Assistance Contract (TAC) from 1994 to 2008.

2003

PSC & Merangin II Block

We signed a Production Sharing Contract (PSC) and were officially appointed as the operator of the Merangin II Block, Musi Rawas Utara, South Sumatera.

2004

Belida PSC Appointment

We signed another PSC and were officially appointed as the operator of the Belida Block, Muara Enim, South Sumatera.

2007

Tampi Oil Discovery

We successfully made an oil discovery at the Tampi Field of the Merangin II Block.

2008

Sungai Anggur Discovery & Mining Expansion

We made another successful oil discovery at the Sungai Anggur Field of the Belida Block.

We also started our coal mining operation through our subsidiary, Solo Raya Mining.

2009

Blora PSC & Industry Recognition

We proudly signed another PSC and were officially appointed as the operator of the Blora Block.

In the same year, we were awarded The Best PSC Exploration Commitment and recognized as one of the PSCs that fulfilled the Work Program and Budget on time.

2010

First Oil Production (Tampi Field)

We successfully completed our first oil production at the Tampi Field of the Merangin II Block, performed through an Early Processing Facility (EPF).

We managed to produce around 1,500–2,400 barrels of crude oil per day in the period of 2010–2018.

2012

Cantik Gas Discovery

We discovered natural gas at the Cantik Field of the Belida Block.

2013

Exploration Commitment Awards

We were proudly acknowledged as one of the PSCs that fulfilled the Exploration Commitment and Hydrocarbon Discovery (Gold Category) for the Belida Block and one of the PSCs that fulfilled the Exploration Commitment (Blue Category) for the Blora Block.

2018

First Oil Production (Sungai Anggur)

We successfully completed our first oil production at the Sungai Anggur Field of the Belida Block, performed through an Early Processing Facility (EPF).

2020

First Gas Production & Rembang Discovery

We completed our first gas production at the Cantik Field of the Belida Block, performed through an EPF.

We also successfully discovered natural gas at the Rembang Field in the same year.

2022

Sungai Anggur Selatan-1 Discovery

Sungai Anggur Selatan-1 well oil and gas discovery. It was noted that it succeeded in obtaining test results of 5,451 Barrels Oil per Day (BPOD), 434 Barrels Condensate per Day (BCPD) and 17,986 MMSCFD from the 4 tests that had been carried out.

2025

Sungai Anggur Selatan-2 Test Results

Sungai Anggur Selatan-2 well oil and gas discovery.It was noted that it succeeded in obtaining test results of 3,856 Barrels Oil per Day (BPOD) and 3,257 MMSCFD.
