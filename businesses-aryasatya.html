Sele Raya Aryasatya

Pioneering Offshore Tin Mining Excellence

Part of the Sele Raya Group, PT Sele Raya Aryasatya combines advanced Thai-engineered technology with local expertise to drive sustainable offshore mining solutions.

OVERVIEW

Precision Offshore Tin Mining & Operations

PT Sele Raya Aryasatya is a premier offshore tin mining company developed by the Sele Raya Group. Established as a key player in the industry, we specialize in high-efficiency subsea mineral extraction.

Since April 2012, we have successfully deployed and operated an advanced Tin Suction Vessel (KIP) in the Kundur Islands, Riau Islands Province. Our operations are conducted under the strict mining authority of PT Timah Indonesia, ensuring that every project aligns with national strategic work plans and world-class industry standards.

Resource Potential & Asset Value

The Amanah Block covers a vast concession area of 1,753.15 square kilometers on the South Sumatran mainland. Extensive geological assessments indicate substantial reserves that offer high commercial value

Advanced Extraction

Utilizing a specialized, Thai-manufactured suction vessel (KIP) to extract high-quality tin ore directly from the seabed.

High-Volume Output

Our streamlined processes enable a robust production capacity, consistently exceeding 25 tonnes of tin ore per month.

Strategic Partnerships

Operating within designated areas under PT Timah, we ensure full regulatory compliance and operational security.
