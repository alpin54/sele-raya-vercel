A Legacy of Innovation and Growth

Sele Raya Group is a leading Indonesia-based company headquartered in Jakarta, with strategic operations across the archipelago. For over five decades, we have played a vital role in Indonesia’s economic journey through five key business lines: Energy, Mining, Agroforestry, FMCG, and Technology.

Our journey began in 1974 as an oilfield services company. Through constant innovation and learning, we evolved into a primary energy producer in 1994, later expanding into natural gas, tin mining, and consumer goods. Today, we are a stronger, more resilient group of companies dedicated to the highest standards of performance.

At Sele Raya, we believe that profit is the result of our commitment. Our focus remains on the planet and its people, ensuring that every project we undertake leaves a positive impact on the environment and society.

BEYOND ENERGY

Investing in a Smarter, Digitally Connected Future.

Sele Raya Group has evolved into a progressive multi-sector leader. While our roots are in energy, our vision extends far beyond. Today, we play a major role in Indonesia and the Southeast Asian region across Energy, Forestry, and the Digital Space.

Our focus is clear: ensuring Indonesia’s energy security while enhancing the well-being of its people. We don't just invest in businesses; we invest in solutions that improve lives.

A Diversified Leader

As a progressive energy company, Sele Raya Group is a key driver of Indonesia’s energy security. Beyond production, we have strategically expanded into Forestry, Technology, and the Digital Space, ensuring our growth directly enhances the well-being of the Indonesian people.

Today, we are more than an energy provider we are a catalyst for digital transformation. Our investment portfolio includes some of the world’s most innovative platforms:

A leading Healthtech platform revolutionizing telemedicine and pharmacy delivery.

A Southeast Asian giant improving transportation and logistical efficiency.

Pushing the boundaries of technology "beyond and to infinity."

Empowering the nation through accessible online education.

A P2P Fintech leader providing SMEs with affordable growth capital.

Digitalizing travel and tourism from Sumatra to Papua.

Authentic sustainable journeys to hidden travel destinations.

Streamlining event management through digital guest solutions.

Providing advanced data infrastructure for global industries.

Helping brands build profitable and long-term customer loyalty.
