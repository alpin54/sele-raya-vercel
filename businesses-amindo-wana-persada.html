Amindo Wana Persada

Premium Meranti, Responsibly Sourced.

Over 2.9 Million m3 of high-quality timber potential, managed under the highest Sustainable Forest Management (PHPL) standards.

A Legacy of Sustainable Forestry
PT Amindo Wana Persada, a proud member of the Sele Raya Group, is a premier forest concession company dedicated to sustainable resource management in Indonesia.

Our concession covers approximately 43,680 hectares in the Berau Regency, East Kalimantan. With a business permit (IUPHHK-HA) valid until October 14, 2054, we offer long-term stability and reliability in the timber and forestry sector.

Commitment to Sustainability

We don't just manage forests; we protect their future. PT Amindo Wana Persada has proudly obtained the Sustainable Forest Management Certification (PHPL). This certification confirms that our operations meet the highest performance standards for sustainable production forest management as endorsed by the Minister of Forestry.

Operational Excellence

Our operations are driven by data and a rich supply of high-quality timber. We focus on delivering premium wood products while maintaining the ecological health of our concession area.

Premium Timber Quality

Our primary stock consists of the highly sought-after Meranti group.

High Yield Potential

Our concession maintains a significant diameter class of > 50 cm, with an average density of 68 m³/Ha.

Massive Reserves

We have an estimated potential of 2,900,688 m³ of standing timber.

Proven Track Record

With a production achievement of 71,000 m³ between 2014 and 2019, we have demonstrated consistent operational capability and growth.

Health, Safety, & Environment

Safety and sustainability are at the core of our corporate DNA. We implement rigorous HSE standards to protect our workforce and the natural ecosystem.

Fire Prevention

We actively manage the "Fire-Care Society" (MPA) and monitor potential hotspots to prevent forest fires.
